About Me

A previous educator who fell in love with coding

I first became engrossed with coding late 2021. Being someone who loves creating, designing, and organizing, a dear friend reccomended trying out Khan Academy's computer programming course. I figured why not and decided to give it a shot. After completing the task of building a snowman - I knew right then and there that I was craving more. Early 2022, I bought a course though Udemy. Soon after, I was using my creativeness by building my own websites through HTML and CSS. From there, I knew I wanted to take my learning a step further and I bought the SheCodes workshops.

Throughout the last few months, while being full-time in the classroom, I have been coding early mornings, late into the evenings, and multiple hours on the weekends. It has been the thing I look forward to each day.

Ultimately, I am looking forward to the day when I land a full-time role in a company that believes in me, like I do myself, where I am able to contribute my skills all the while growing with a team.
