Fly-fishing App

Over the last few years, fly fishing has become one of my favorite hobbies. For my SheCodes Basic project, I wanted to create a website that was geared towards women. According to Recreational Boating & Fishing Foundation, only 36% of females partake in the hobby. I wanted my website to peak an interest in women who have been curious, yet hesitant, to take that leap into the sport.

To create the project, I used: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

For my SheCodes Plus project, I created a weather app that uses a live API, which allows you to pull up the weather for your current location or you can type in a specific location, as well as the 5-day future forecast.

To create the project, I used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.
